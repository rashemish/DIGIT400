BIO:

Hi! My name is Raashee Mishra, and I am a Digital Media, Arts, and Technology student in my senior year of undergrad at Penn State Behrend. For my research project in my Digital Project Production class, I am researching Data Privacy and how it affects the careers of designers, such as myself.

PROPOSAL:

For my topic, I have chosen Data Privacy. Specifically, how the importance and gravity of data privacy is slowly dwindling in the UI/UX design world in order to advance the efficiency of UI/UX design towards users. I want to be able to explain several aspects of this topic through analyzing data privacy breach cases, understanding how to incorporate ethical design practices from guides in my own projects, and argumentative essays about data privacy in general.
